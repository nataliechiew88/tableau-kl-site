About Tableau KL

Flowers are not
decoration. They are feeling,
made visible.

"We believe every arrangement is a composition — studied, intentional, and emotionally alive."

Tableau KL is a modern luxury floral atelier based in Kuala Lumpur. Founded with a deep love for artistic beauty and meaningful gifting, Tableau exists for those who understand that what we give says what words cannot. Every stem is chosen. Every arrangement is named. Nothing leaves the atelier without our care.

Reach out via WhatsApp

Tell us the occasion, the person, the palette you have in mind. We respond personally within the hour.
