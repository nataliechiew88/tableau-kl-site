"Le Jardin asks: what if wildness could be art? Our answer is this — it always was."

The Atelier Bespoke service is for those who need something entirely their own. A colour we don't have on our list. A scale we haven't offered. A feeling that can't be named yet. Brief us. We compose from there.

Bespoke orders are taken for individual gifts, corporate gifting in volume, weddings, events, and brand activations. Lead time varies — WhatsApp us to begin.

How Bespoke Works
I
Brief us

Share the occasion, the feeling, the colour references, and the budget. WhatsApp or email — whichever you prefer.

II
We propose

Within 24 hours we'll send you a proposal: blooms, palette, size, and pricing. No obligation to proceed.

III
We compose

On confirmation and deposit, we source and arrange your bespoke piece to the agreed brief. Nothing is off-the-shelf.

IV
Delivered to you

Delivered in Tableau packaging within the agreed timeframe. For large volume orders, we coordinate logistics with you directly.
